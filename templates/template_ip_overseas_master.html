Master Agreement

Article 1 Definitions

1. "Game IP" means all intellectual property rights owned or lawfully controlled by Licensor in and to the game identified in the Detail Sheet, including rules, mechanics, artwork, packaging, brand assets, and related materials. 2. "Territory" and "Language" mean the territory and language specified in the Detail Sheet. 3. "Localized Product" means the product localized for the Territory and Language in accordance with this Agreement. 4. "Supplemental Terms" means any schedule, addendum, or other written supplement executed by both Parties and expressly incorporated into this Agreement. 5. "Applicable Supplemental Terms" means the Supplemental Terms, if any, identified in the Detail Sheet or later made applicable by written amendment signed by both Parties.

Article 2 Grant of Rights

Subject to this Agreement, including any Applicable Supplemental Terms, Licensor grants to Licensee, during the Term and within the Territory and Language, the rights, to the extent applicable to the transaction model agreed by the Parties, to translate, localize, reproduce, market, distribute, and sell the Localized Product, and to use Licensor's trademarks and brand assets solely as reasonably necessary for those purposes. If Exclusivity is selected in the Detail Sheet, the grant shall be exclusive within the designated Territory and Language, except for any rights expressly reserved by Licensor in writing. All rights not expressly granted are reserved to Licensor.

Article 3 Basic Principles of Localization

Licensee shall localize the Game IP faithfully and with commercially reasonable editorial quality consistent with industry standards for analog game publishing in the Territory. No material change may be made to gameplay, game balance, core rules text, artwork, packaging identity, or brand presentation without Licensor's prior written approval or deemed approval under this Agreement. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to maintain production timelines.

Article 4 Ownership and License in Localization Materials

As between the Parties, and except for the underlying Game IP and Licensor Materials, all right, title, and interest in and to translations, editorial adaptations, glossaries, layout files, and other localization materials specifically created by or for Licensee for the Localized Product remain vested in Licensee or its contractors, subject to the rights granted to Licensor. Licensee grants to Licensor a worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, transferable, sublicensable, royalty-free license to use the Localization Materials in connection with the Game IP.

Article 5 Allocation of Responsibility

Licensor is responsible for the unmodified Game IP and source materials originally supplied by Licensor. Licensee is responsible, at its own cost and risk, for localization execution, local commercialization, local labeling, importation, marketplace compliance, and product compliance obligations in the Territory, except to the extent directly caused by unmodified source materials supplied by Licensor or Licensor's written instructions.

Article 6 Representations, Warranties, and Indemnification

Each Party represents and warrants that it has full corporate power and authority to enter into and perform this Agreement. Licensor represents that it owns or lawfully controls the Game IP rights necessary to grant the rights expressly granted herein. Licensee represents that it will conduct localization, marketing, sale, distribution, reporting, and other activities under this Agreement in compliance with applicable laws. Each Party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims to the extent arising from its own breach, non-compliance, or unauthorized modifications.

Article 7 Payments, Taxes, and Set-Off

Unless otherwise expressly stated in the Applicable Supplemental Terms, all amounts payable under this Agreement are exclusive of VAT, GST, sales tax, or similar indirect taxes. If a Party is required by applicable law to withhold tax from any payment under this Agreement, that Party may do so, provided that it promptly notifies the receiving Party and furnishes reasonable evidence of remittance. Any overdue payment accrues interest at one percent (1.0%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower.

Article 8 Term and Termination

This Agreement takes effect on the Effective Date and continues for the Initial Term specified in the Detail Sheet. If Renewal is selected, this Agreement renews automatically unless either Party gives notice of non-renewal within the period stated in the Detail Sheet. Upon expiration or termination other than for Licensee's material breach, Licensee may sell off existing inventory during the Sell-Off Period stated in the Detail Sheet, subject to continued compliance with Licensor's then-current brand guidelines and any reasonable channel restrictions.

Article 9 General Provisions

Any notice under this Agreement may be delivered by email to the Notice Contacts set out in the Detail Sheet. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Japan, and any dispute shall be submitted to the Tokyo District Court as the court of first instance unless JCAA arbitration is timely elected by a non-Japan party. This Agreement consists of the Detail Sheet, this Master Agreement, and any Applicable Supplemental Terms. If Japanese and English versions are executed, the English version prevails unless otherwise agreed in writing.
